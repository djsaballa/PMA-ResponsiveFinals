Three Ways to Apply

  • ONLINE

    For online application, applicants must scan the following documents and attach the files in pdf format to the Online Application System.

    – High School From 137 or 138

    – NSO copy of Birth Certificate

  • HAND CARRY

    Applicants can also personally bring their application form to this address:

    Office of Cadet Admission
    Philippine Military Academy
    Fort del Pilar, Baguio City
    2602

  • MAIL

    Applicants must open, print and fill-up the application form and send it via mail to the following address:

    Office of Cadet Admission
    Philippine Military Academy
    Fort del Pilar, Baguio City
    2602

For those interested to take the PMA Entrance Exam, here are the qualifications and requirements.

Qualifications

  • Natural Born Filipino Citizen.
  • Physically fit and of good moral character
  • Single and has never been married
  • Must pass the PMA Entrance Examination
  • No Administrative / Criminal Case
  • At least High School Graduate with at least 85% GPA
    • or must graduate not later than June of the year following the date of examination taken for Grade 12 students
  • Height Requirement for both Male and Female is 5 feet (not to exceed 6’4″ for both).
  • At least 17 years old but not a day older than 22 years on the reporting date (01 June 2020).

Medical Disqualifications

  • color blind
  • any communicable disease
  • physical deformities
  • scoliosis
  • tattoos
  • other medical conditions as determined by AFP Medical Board Nr 1

Requirements

  • Duly accomplished application form
  • 2 pcs. of 2×2 ID Picture (white background)
  • Photocopy of PSA Birth Certificate
  • Photocopy of Form 137/138 or copy of latest grades
