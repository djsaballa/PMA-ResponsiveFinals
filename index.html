About Us

The Philippine Military Academy is the
premier military academy for Filipinos aspiring
for a commission as a military officer of
the Armed Forces of the Philippines (AFP).

History

An Officer's School of the Philippine Constabulary
was established on February 17, 1905 within the
walls of Intramuros in Manila.[15] This school was
relocated to Baguio on September 1, 1908, at Camp
Henry Allen where it would stay for many years to come.
